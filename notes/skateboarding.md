# common setups:
- park skateboard -> 
	- high (98a-101a)
	- low (54mm)
## Powell Peralta Nano Cubics


- cruiser ->
	- soft (78a)
	- low (54-60mm)
		- Keep this on the lower end to avoid wheelbite (and to avoid a high board)
	- wide (contact patch: 40-50mm)
## Landyachtz Fatty Hawgs


- longboard ->
	- soft (under 90a)
	- high (above 70mm)
	- wide (contact patch: above 40mm)
